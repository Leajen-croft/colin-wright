Colin William Wright

Memorial Site

07/08/1947 - 18/08/2015

Colin William Wright passed away suddenly on 18/08/2015. He was a wonderful father, brother and friend to many, all over the world.

This site is a project in progress as a memorial to him. It is planned to create a photographic record of his life together with a written biography. Also we would like to include eulogies from those who knew him.

If you knew Colin and would like to send your condolences or share any stories/photos of your time spent him please send them to contact@colin-wright.co.uk
